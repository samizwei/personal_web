Bio

I hold a Master’s degree in Physics with research interests in condensed matter theory and computational physics as well as machine learning. My work focuses on variational methods, including Variational Monte Carlo (VMC), Neural Quantum States (NQS), Density Matrix Renormalization Group (DMRG), and Density Functional Theory (DFT). I conducted my master thesis under the supervision of Prof. Juan Carrasquilla investigating a triangular quantum antiferromagnet using neural quantum states and VMC. Besides academic research, I have experience in industry as an R&D intern as Sensirion AG, working on TC sensors as well as as an acturial intern at Zurich Insurance.
